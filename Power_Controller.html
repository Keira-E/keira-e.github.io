Project Overview

Designed, fabricated, and validated a custom Printed Circuit Board (PCB) to power and control an AI-driven underwater computer vision system for monitoring fish behavior. This complex board integrates an STM32 microcontroller for primary control, multiple DC-DC converters for efficient power regulation, UART/I2C communication interfaces, and high-voltage LED drivers for imaging. Significant effort was dedicated to EMI mitigation and thermal management to ensure reliable operation in harsh industrial environments.

Key Features & Technologies

  • Custom PCB Design (KiCAD)
  • STM32 Microcontroller
  • Power Electronics (DC-DC Converters)
  • EMI Mitigation & Thermal Management
  • Serial Communication (UART, I2C)
  • High-Voltage LED Drivers
  • Edge AI & Computer Vision
