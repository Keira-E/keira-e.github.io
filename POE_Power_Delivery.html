Project Overview

Developed a robust Power over Ethernet (PoE) system compliant with the IEEE 802.3bt standard. This system is engineered for resilient, distributed AI deployments, enabling safe and efficient power delivery up to 100 meters over a single Ethernet cable. A key innovation is its localized AC/DC conversion and supervisory control, which enhances the reliability and scalability of edge AI hardware in challenging environments.

Key Features & Technologies

  • Power over Ethernet (PoE) - IEEE 802.3bt
  • Power Electronics (AC/DC Conversion)
  • Edge AI Hardware Systems
  • Supervisory Control Systems
  • High-Reliability Power Delivery
  • Distributed Systems Design
