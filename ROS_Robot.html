Project Overview

As the team leader in a group of four, I guided the design and construction of an autonomous robot for a competitive challenge. The robot was required to use ROS (Robot Operating System) for AI-driven path planning to navigate a complex obstacle course, and then detect, pick up, and move various objects. Our final solution, which was voted "Most Innovative Design," utilized an ARM-based microcontroller and featured a custom chassis designed in SolidWorks and 3D printed for rapid prototyping.

Key Features & Technologies

  • Team Leadership
  • Robot Operating System (ROS)
  • AI Path Planning
  • ARM Microcontroller
  • 3D Printing & Mechanical Design (SolidWorks)
  • Object Detection & Manipulation
